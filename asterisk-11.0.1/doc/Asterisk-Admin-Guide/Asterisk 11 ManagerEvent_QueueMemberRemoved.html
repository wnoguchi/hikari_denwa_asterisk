QueueMemberRemoved

Synopsis

Raised when a member is removed from the queue.

Syntax

Event: QueueMemberRemoved
Queue: <value>
Location: <value>
MemberName: <value>
Queue: <value>
Location: <value>
MemberName: <value>
Arguments
  • Queue - The name of the queue.
  • Location - The queue member's channel technology or location.
  • MemberName - The name of the queue member.
