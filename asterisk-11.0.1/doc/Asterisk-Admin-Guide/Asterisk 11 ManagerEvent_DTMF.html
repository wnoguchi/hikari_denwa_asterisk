DTMF

Synopsis

Raised when a DTMF digit has started or ended on a channel.

Syntax

Event: DTMF
Direction: <value>
Begin: <value>
End: <value>
Channel: <value>
Uniqueid: <value>
Digit: <value>
Arguments
  • Direction
    • Received
    • Sent
  • Begin
    • Yes
    • No
  • End
    • Yes
    • No
  • Channel
  • Uniqueid
  • Digit
