QueueMemberRinginuse

Synopsis

Raised when a member's ringinuse setting is changed.

Syntax

Event: QueueMemberRinginuse
Queue: <value>
Location: <value>
Ringinuse: <value>
Queue: <value>
Location: <value>
Arguments
  • Queue - The name of the queue.
  • Location - The queue member's channel technology or location.
